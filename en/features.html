Rudy

A colleague who never
takes a day off.

Rudy is the AI assistant that lives inside Rumo. He knows your hotel, your procedures and your data. Ask a question in plain language and Rudy gives you the answer. No manual needed, no searching around. Just ask.

  • "Which rooms are available tomorrow?"
  • "How much revenue did we make this month?"
  • "What's our cancellation policy?"
  • Knows your reservations, your numbers and your handbook articles
  • Always available, always patient

And more

Everything you'd expect from a PMS.

The essentials are all here. Every single one.

Reservations & Calendar

Visual calendar with drag-and-drop. Multi-room bookings, per-room status, quotes and full booking history.

Channel Manager

Booking.com, Airbnb, Expedia and more. Rates and availability are automatically synced via Channex.

Booking Engine

Direct bookings through your own website, commission-free. With referrer tracking and allotments for partners.

Invoicing & Peppol

Invoices, credit notes and proformas with correct VAT. Peppol e-invoicing and UBL 2.1 XML export built in.

Payments

Online payment links, VCC charging, pre-authorisations. Bancontact, Visa, Mastercard. Automatically linked to invoices.

Guest Portal

Guests check in online, confirm quotes, pay and fill in their ID details. On their own phone.

Housekeeping

Real-time room status. Cleaning staff work from their phone, updates appear instantly in the calendar.

F&B

Breakfast lists, minibar and restaurant charges. Automatically added to the room bill, linked to the rate plan.

Communication

Email templates per event, sent automatically in your guest's language. Multilingual: NL, FR, DE, EN.

Rates & Allotments

Room types, rate plans, cancellation policies and allotments for partners. Pushed to all channels.

Reports

Occupancy, ADR, RevPAR, revenue per channel. Central invoice overview. Export to CSV, Excel or PDF.

Guest Profiles

Automatically built from reservations. Guest, booker and company profiles with VIES VAT validation.
