About us

Built by a hotelier and an engineer.

Rumo isn't a product from some big software factory. It's made by two people who believe hotel software can be better. One who stands behind the front desk every day, and one who knows how to turn that into software.

Jeffrey

Product & Design

Jeffrey runs two hotels in Belgium: Hotel Chamade (51 rooms) and Carlton Hotel (22 rooms), both three-star. He knows how a front desk operates, what goes wrong with check-ins at 11 PM, and why most PMS systems are too complicated for what they do.

He designs Rumo based on what he needs every day. No features that look good on a slide, but functions that are actually used by real receptionists.

Seba

Technology & Architecture

Seba works in an innovation team in the tech industry and builds software that scales. He's responsible for everything under the hood: the backend, security, infrastructure, and integrations.

He makes sure Rumo is fast, secure, and reliable. Multi-tenant architecture, real-time synchronisation, Peppol e-invoicing, channel manager connectivity. The technical foundation that keeps everything running.

Why Rumo

Hotel software that wasn't built in an office.

Jeffrey has worked in hotels for years and always missed a system that truly matched how his team works. Something fast, clear, and that doesn't require a three-day training course. So he decided to build it himself.

Together with Seba, he's building a PMS that focuses on what small and mid-sized hotels actually need. Nothing more. Nothing less. No 400 features of which you use 12. Just software that works the way you think.

How we think

Three principles.

Simplicity above all

If a receptionist doesn't get it within 10 seconds, it's not good enough. Every click, every screen, every label is weighed on simplicity.

Built for real hotels

No theoretical features. Everything in Rumo is tested in a working hotel. If it doesn't work at 7 AM during a check-out rush, it doesn't belong.

Honest and transparent

No hidden costs. No annual commitment. No hour-long sales calls. What you see is what you get. And if Rumo isn't for you, we'll tell you that too.
