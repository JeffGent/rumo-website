How does it work?

Rumo Pay processes payments directly to your account. Guests pay through a secure payment page, from a payment request, the booking engine, or the guest portal.

Payouts are made automatically to your bank account. You can track everything in Rumo: which guest paid, when, and for which reservation.

In case of a chargeback (disputed payment), the disputed amount + a one-time dispute fee of €15 is temporarily held on your account. If you win the dispute, everything is released. In your Rumo dashboard you can always see exactly which amounts are held and why.

No monthly fees. No minimum volumes. No contracts. You only pay when a guest pays.
